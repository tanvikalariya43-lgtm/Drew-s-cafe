Our Story

Founded in 2035, Drew's Café began as a small neighborhood coffee shop with a simple mission: to serve the freshest coffee and most delicious food in a warm, welcoming environment. What started as a dream between two friends has grown into a beloved community gathering spot.

Our passion for quality ingredients, artisanal brewing methods, and creating meaningful connections with our customers drives everything we do. We source our beans from sustainable farms and work with local bakeries to bring you the best flavors our community has to offer.
