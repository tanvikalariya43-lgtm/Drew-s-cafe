Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations for parties of 6 or more. For smaller groups, we operate on a first-come, first-served basis.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options. Check our menu or ask our staff for recommendations.

Is there parking available?

We have limited street parking available. There's also a paid parking garage two blocks away on Franklin Street.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide catering for events and offices. Please contact us with your requirements for a custom quote.

Are you hiring?

We're always looking for passionate people to join our team. Please send your resume to careers@drewscafe.com.

Do you have WiFi?

Yes, we offer free WiFi for our customers. Ask our staff for the password when you visit.
